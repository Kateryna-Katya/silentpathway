Arrow Pipe – Escape Puzzle

Unblock the Grid. Free the Flow.

Escape the tangled maze in this puzzle that'll really make you think.
In Arrow Pipe, every move matters. Plan carefully. Slide components along their paths. Avoid collisions. Clear the grid and let the water flow freely.
Starts off pretty chill, but man, it gets tough fast - you really gotta think ahead. Narrow gaps, tight spaces, and complex arrangements demand focus and foresight.
Clean design. Fluid movement. It's just really satisfying when you nail a tricky puzzle.

How to Play

Precision in Every Swipe

One wrong move can block the drainage. Every correct move feels satisfying.
The mechanics are simple but rewarding:

  • Tap and slide components along their assigned path

  • Avoid moving pieces into collisions

  • Navigate narrow gaps carefully

  • Clear the board to allow the water to flow

Escape Mechanics

Plan Before You Move

This game basically punishes you for being impatient - you've gotta think three moves ahead or you're screwed.
This is not about speed — it’s about smart decisions. As levels progress, you’ll face:

  • Tighter spaces

  • More complex layouts

  • Limited movement options

  • Increased planning depth

Difficulty Progression

Calm Start.
Intense Challenge.

You'll pick it up pretty quick, and it feels good when you start getting better.
The early stages ease you into the mechanics, allowing you to understand the flow system. Then gradually:

  • Grids become more compact

  • Mistakes become more punishing

  • Escape routes become less obvious
